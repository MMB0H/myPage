I'm Pixel, Scottish Fold cat

The Scottish Fold is a unique and adorable cat breed known for its distinctive folded ears. These cats have a round face, large eyes, and a sweet expression that makes them look like eternal kittens. They are known for their friendly and affectionate nature, making them excellent companions. Scottish Folds are playful and enjoy interactive games, but they also love cuddling and lounging around the house. Their coat can be short or long, and it comes in a variety of colors and patterns. Despite their cute appearance, they are generally healthy and robust, although they can be prone to certain genetic conditions. Regular vet check-ups and a balanced diet are essential for their well-being. These cats are adaptable and can live comfortably in both apartments and houses. They tend to get along well with other pets and children, making them a great addition to any family. Overall, the Scottish Fold is a delightful breed that brings joy and companionship to their owners.

I'm Plush, British Longhair cat

The British Longhair cat is a medium to large-sized feline with a robust and muscular build. It has a broad chest, strong legs, and a well-rounded body. Its most striking feature is its long, dense coat, which is soft and plush to the touch. The fur around its neck forms a distinctive ruff, adding to its majestic appearance. British Longhairs come in a variety of colors and patterns, including solid, tabby, and bicolor. Their round, expressive eyes are typically copper or gold, though blue and green eyes are also possible. These cats have a calm and easygoing temperament, making them excellent companions for families and individuals alike. They are known for being affectionate, yet they also appreciate their independence. Regular grooming is essential to maintain their luxurious coat and prevent matting. Overall, the British Longhair is a beautiful, gentle, and loving breed that can bring joy to any home.

About me

I was born on August 16, 2018 in a large family of 5 kittens. It was in Russia, the city of St. Petersburg. I had fun with my brothers and sisters. We played, I explored the world. But when some bipeds came, I hid. I was afraid of these big people. They came to the human mother and took my brothers and sisters. And one day they took me too. I moved into a large apartment with two people. In the first days I was afraid of everything and settled in the bathroom. I was 3 months old, I was already eating pate and going to the litter box myself. After a couple of days I got used to it and found out how big my apartment was now. I studied everything, ran and played. I felt good. that's how I grew up. I was loved. after a couple of years, I was given to some other people for a couple of months. but I don’t understand why. Then they came back for me and I immediately made the trip on the plane. I was scared, but I bravely endured this day. Now I live in a spacious apartment in Israel. I feel good here, even though it’s hot. I have a balcony and sometimes I chase birds there.
